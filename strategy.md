## High-Leverage Service Recommendations

Based on your background (AI PM, 6 production systems, voice/CV/NLP experience) and market gaps:

### 1. AI Voice Agent Implementation
**Your edge:** You built Riya Voice Platform handling 40K leads/month with Hinglish code-switching.
**Ideal customer:** Indian B2C companies with high call volume (real estate, edtech, fintech, healthtech).
**Problem:** Manual call centers are expensive. Generic AI voices don't handle Indian languages/context.
**Price:** ₹1,50,000 - 3,00,000 per deployment.
**Timeline:** 7-14 days.
**Difficulty:** Medium.
**Competition:** High at commodity level, low at Indian-language + HITL level.
**Acquisition:** LinkedIn outreach to CXOs at Series A/B Indian startups. Demo calls.

### 2. Internal AI Assistant / Chatbot
**Your edge:** You built production RAG systems and HITL platforms.
**Ideal customer:** 50-500 person companies with knowledge management pain (SaaS, consultancies, law firms).
**Problem:** Employees waste 3-5 hours/week searching for internal docs. No one reads the wiki.
**Price:** ₹75,000 - 2,00,000 per deployment.
**Timeline:** 5-10 days.
**Difficulty:** Low-Medium.
**Competition:** High with generic chatbot builders, low with custom RAG + PII-aware implementations.
**Acquisition:** Reddit (r/saas, r/startups), IndieHackers, LinkedIn posts showing before/after.

### 3. AI Workflow Automation
**Your edge:** You run n8n, Coolify, Supabase yourself. You understand infra + product.
**Ideal customer:** Operations-heavy startups (marketplaces, D2C, logistics).
**Problem:** Manual data entry, lead routing, report generation eating 20+ hours/week.
**Price:** ₹50,000 - 1,50,000 per workflow system.
**Timeline:** 3-7 days.
**Difficulty:** Low.
**Competition:** High from generic agencies, but you can combine PM + engineering.
**Acquisition:** Twitter/X threads showing automation builds. Hacker News "Show HN" posts.

### 4. AI Feature Prioritization Sprint
**Your edge:** FeatureFit tool + IIM Indore PM training + 6 production systems.
**Ideal customer:** Seed-Series A founders who don't have a PM yet.
**Problem:** Building features no one wants. No framework for deciding what to build next.
**Price:** ₹25,000 - 75,000 per sprint (includes FeatureFit analysis + 2 workshops + roadmap).
**Timeline:** 3-5 days.
**Difficulty:** Low.
**Competition:** Low. Most consultants do generic "product strategy." You bring an AI tool + specific framework.
**Acquisition:** YC founder directories, LinkedIn, Twitter contrarian posts about feature bloat.

### 5. LLM Fine-tuning & Prompt Engineering
**Your edge:** LLM-Tuner repos, production NER models, VLM work.
**Ideal customer:** Companies with domain-specific data who get garbage from GPT-4.
**Problem:** Off-the-shelf LLMs hallucinate on their domain. Need custom models.
**Price:** ₹1,00,000 - 2,50,000 per model.
**Timeline:** 10-20 days.
**Difficulty:** High.
**Competition:** Medium. Many ML engineers, few who combine PM + LLM + production systems.
**Acquisition:** Technical blog posts, GitHub repos, Hacker News.
