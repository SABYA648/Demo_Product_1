## If You're Stuck

**No replies?**
- Your subject line is too generic. Make it specific and slightly uncomfortable.
- Your message is too long. Cut it by 50%.
- You're not personalizing enough. Mention one specific thing about them.

**Conversations but no closes?**
- Your price is too low. Raising prices signals confidence.
- You're not asking for the sale. End every conversation with "Should I send the proposal?"
- You're solving a pain they don't feel urgently. Find the urgent pain.

**No time?**
- Batch outreach: 30 minutes in the morning, 30 minutes at night.
- Use templates but personalize the first sentence.
- Content creation: write 1 post, repurpose it into 3 formats (LinkedIn, Twitter, Reddit).
