## Personalization Framework

For every outreach, fill in:

1. **Company name** and **founder name** (exact spelling)
2. **Industry** (SaaS, fintech, edtech, etc.)
3. **Specific pain point** (from their website, LinkedIn posts, or job listings)
4. **Relevant metric** (from your case studies)
5. **Specific observation** (their recent funding, launch, hiring, or post)

**Rule:** Never send the same message twice. At minimum, change the pain point and the observation.

**Volume targets:**
- Week 1: 20 LinkedIn connection requests + 10 cold emails
- Week 2: 30 LinkedIn + 20 emails + 5 Reddit comments
- Week 3: 40 LinkedIn + 30 emails + 10 Reddit + 5 Twitter DMs
- Week 4+: 50+ outreach per week until 5 qualified conversations

**Conversion expectations:**
- 10% connection acceptance rate on LinkedIn
- 2-3% reply rate on cold email
- 1% conversion to call
- Target: 50 outreach = 1 qualified conversation
